Music Tape
Overview
Music Tape is my passion project, a full-stack web application designed to create a vibrant music-sharing experience. As a music app, it enables users to register and upload their singles and albums for others to enjoy. With a focus on user personalization, Music Tape allows users to upload their profile picture and cover picture, making their presence on the platform uniquely their own. Additionally, they can upload cover pictures for individual songs and albums, enhancing the visual appeal and identity of their musical creations. A standout feature of Music Tape is the album page, where the background dynamically changes to match the colors of the album cover photo. This design element adds a touch of creativity and immersion to the user's browsing experience.
